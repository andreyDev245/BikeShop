More about the product

The Cronus Dynamic 520 bicycle on 27.5 "wheels is a classic mountain hardtail, which in our latitudes is used as the most versatile bike. And not for nothing, because it allows you to confidently ride many kilometers on asphalt and on the ground. The bike is assembled on an aluminum frame and spring-oil fork, has 30 gears and powerful disc hydraulic brakes.The light frame of the hardtail is made of a durable aluminum alloy, which is not inferior to steel in reliability, but lighter. By using the butting technology of the pipes, the excess weight of the eyelet has been eliminated. It consists in the fact that the pipes have thinned walls in areas with low loads. This distribution of material reduces weight without loss in strength. Also, the frame is hydroformed. complex profile that provides high torsional rigidity awn. And the bike owes her when we talk about coasting and dynamics.

Also in this frame we have internal cable routing and tapered head tube. Internal wiring will help keep the transmission cables clean and intact. And this is the key to a clear gear change. The tapered head tube gives high torsional rigidity to the control unit. Due to this, the bike drives perfectly.
