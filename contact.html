about our company

The company "Veliki.com.ua" was founded on November 20, 2007. We focused on excellent customer service and assortment. We have spared no expense and time to improve the site and the level of consultation. Good service requires investment, so we abandoned the price struggle and dumping that dominated the then e-commerce world. In the early years, it was difficult to survive among the mass of one-day online stores, which, apart from the lowered price, could not offer either quality service or guarantees. As we expected, our efforts were appreciated by the buyers and this gave us the opportunity to develop!
